Brand Story

Engineered by a sales professional with a keen interest in personal care products, Wild Organic was established in late December 2018. Mr. Jitendra realised that these current products are not the ones he wishes to use. Enlightened by this learning he has launched an organic skincare brand named Wild Organic that produces products made with natural substances. All Wild Organic products are free from SLS/SLES, Sulphates, Artificial preservatives & fragrances. Wild Organic in its purest version is one step closer towards the mother’s nature and further steps away from all chemically infused goods. It’s a small endeavour in calling off all the “toxins” and leading a healthy yet affordable lifestyle.
